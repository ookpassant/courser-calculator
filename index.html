Smart Search

Tell it what you want in plain English. It finds the pairs in your stable that can get you there.

Smart Search looks through your stable, so it needs some horses first.

Import a CSV, or add one by hand.

Tip: Put carries (or carrying) before a recessive to find pairs whose foals carry it without showing it. Good for stacking nfe toward fefe. Works for: filigree, pearl, ether, flaxen, starfield, lacquer, sepulchered, patn.

Scroll Generator

Got a custom scroll and no idea what to make? Let it roll you something.

Staring at a blank scroll? Pick a rarity and it rolls you a design.

How a roll works: a coat colour at that rarity, one marking, modifier, or carrier (that rarity or lower), a 1-in-10 shot at an anomaly, a 1-in-20 shot at a variant, and the temperament you pick.

Chimera Calculator

Got a foal that rolled Chimera? See what could show up in the patch. Enter genotypes by hand, or jump here from a foal card.

Working out a Chimera patch: drop in the foal's genotype and both parents', and it lists the coats the patch could be.

The rule of thumb: a patch can show anything the two parents could produce, minus anomalies neither parent carries.
